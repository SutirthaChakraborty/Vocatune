What is VocaTune?

VocaTune is a revolutionary instrument that combines gesture control and voice to create real-time music. Using gesture recognition and pitch detection, VocaTune transforms hand movements and vocal input into dynamic musical outputs, making music creation intuitive, engaging, and highly expressive.
